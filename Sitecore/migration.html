• 🚀 Sitecore Content Migration: Do’s, Don’ts & MVP Lessons

    As a #SitecoreMVP, I’ve led content migrations that ranged from legacy CMS platforms to modern headless architectures like Sitecore XM Cloud. What I’ve learned is simple but crucial: content migration isn’t just about moving data — it’s a strategic transformation.

    ✅ Key Do’s:
    • Audit existing content. Not everything should be migrated.
    • Define content types and taxonomies before development.
    • Automate the migration process using Sitecore PowerShell, CLI, or third-party tools.
    • Test in a staging environment — validate structure and rendering.
    • Align developers, authors, and marketers early.
    ❌ Common Don’ts:
    • Don’t treat it as a copy-paste job — personalize and modernize.
    • Don’t forget SEO — redirects and metadata must be preserved.
    • Don’t migrate everything — quality beats quantity.
    • Don’t underestimate QA and post-migration governance.
    • Don’t ignore the content author experience — Horizon/Editor must be usable.

    Content migration is more than a technical necessity — it’s your platform’s foundation for future scalability and digital maturity. Each project I’ve worked on through the MVP program has reinforced this lesson: clean, structured, strategic content is your biggest digital asset.
